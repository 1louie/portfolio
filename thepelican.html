THE PELICAN

This is another Bloc project intended for users to be able to crowdfund for charitites and foundations, and allows them to donate to a specific cause or even a product. This site invites two types of users; the crowdfunders and the donors. The tasks for this site is to showcase a fundraiser’s information, highlighting donations, describe

a situation, include images of a particular cause and for donors to search and donate with or without logging into the site. My goal was to design a simple, informative, and search-friendly site. I have included a sitemap, basic wireframes, and color designed site layout including my logo and color palette preferences.
