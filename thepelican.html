THE PELICAN

This is another Bloc project intended for users to be able to crowdfund for charities and foundations, allowing them to donate to a specific cause or product, with or without logging into the site. The Pelican is geared towards two types of users: the crowdfunders and the donors. The objective is to

provide a fundraiser’s information including pictures, cause description, and donation highlights. My goal was to design a simple, informative, and search-friendly site. I included a sitemap, basic wireframes, and a color designed site layout, including my logo and color palette preferences.
